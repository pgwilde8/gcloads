Does your AI work with my current load board?

Yes. You provide the map (DAT, Truckstop, or TruckSmarter), and our AI "Scout" provides the driver. The Scout Extension reads the load data and sends it to our backend for Sarah or Marcus to begin outreach.

What if I only have a DOT number and no MC yet?

No problem. Our system is Authority Neutral. Whether you identify by MC or DOT, we track your activity the same way.

Do I need a special computer?

No, but the AI Scout requires a laptop or tablet to run the Chrome/Edge extension. You can still manage everything and accept loads from your smartphone via our mobile-first dashboard.

Is there a monthly subscription fee?

No. Pay only when you get paid. We charge a flat 2.5% on completed, funded loads. Billing is aggregated weekly.

What is the 2.5% fee?

When you complete a load and get funded via factoring, we collect a 2.5% dispatch service fee. No upfront costs, no monthly minimums. You pay only on loads that pay you.
